Neuro-Voice Awaaz 2.0 — Multi-Modal Assistive System with Signal Processing & Intelligent Response
PUBLICATION NO.
IN202631019926 A1
FILING DATE
20 February 2026
PUBLISHED
10 April 2026
APPLICANT
GNIT Kolkata
Abstract: A multi-modal, voice-enabled embedded assistive system that acquires physiological signals (EEG, ECG, EMG, EOG) or simulated equivalents via analog inputs, preprocesses and denoises the signals, extracts representative features, and applies preconfigured rule-based logic to detect abnormal conditions or infer user intent. Upon detection, the system generates immediate voice-based alerts and records events for offline analysis. Designed for low-resource environments.
Inventors: Dr. Ipsita Saha • Dr. Srabani Biswas • Mrs. Sayani Chandra • Priya Sharma • Sauvik Sarkar • Md. Danish Irfan • Sardar Hanshraj Singh Badal • Adarsh Ujjawal
